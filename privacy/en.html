Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Lyrha ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered voice calling service for elderly care.

This policy is designed to comply with GDPR (European Union), CCPA/CPRA (California, USA), and Korean Personal Information Protection Act.

2.2 Voice and Call Data

Important: Our service records voice calls. This includes voice recordings, transcriptions, call metadata, and AI analysis. Voice data is classified as sensitive personal information under GDPR and Korean PIPA.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Service Delivery: Initiating calls, generating summaries, sending notifications
  • Service Improvement: Training AI models, analyzing usage patterns
  • Communication: Service notifications, support requests
  • Legal Compliance: Complying with legal obligations

6. Your Rights

All users have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your data
  • Portability: Receive your data in a portable format

To exercise your rights, contact us at privacy@lyrha.com.

7. Data Security

  • Encryption: All data encrypted in transit (TLS 1.3) and at rest (AES-256)
  • Access Control: Role-based access with multi-factor authentication
  • Monitoring: 24/7 security monitoring
  • Audits: Regular security audits and penetration testing

8. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.
