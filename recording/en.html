Call Recording Consent

Important Notice: Lyrha records voice calls made through our AI-powered elderly care service. By using our Service and agreeing to this consent, you acknowledge and agree to the recording practices described below.

1. What We Record

Data TypeDescription
Voice AudioComplete audio recordings of AI-initiated calls
TranscriptionsText conversions of spoken conversations
Call MetadataDate, time, duration, and call status
AI AnalysisSentiment indicators, key topics, wellness signals

2. Purpose of Recording

Service Delivery

  • Call Summaries: Generate reports for family members about call content
  • Wellness Monitoring: Identify indicators about the elderly person's wellbeing
  • Missed Information: Allow family members to review call details

Service Improvement

  • AI Training: Improve the quality and naturalness of AI conversations
  • Quality Assurance: Ensure calls meet our service standards

3. Data Storage & Security

  • All recordings stored on secure, encrypted servers (AES-256)
  • Access limited to essential personnel only
  • Multi-factor authentication required

Retention Period - While Account Active

DataRetention
Voice recordingsUntil account deletion
TranscriptionsUntil account deletion
Call summariesUntil account deletion

After Account Deletion

DataProcessing
Voice recordingsAnonymized and retained for AI training
TranscriptionsAnonymized and retained for AI training
Call summariesAnonymized and retained for AI training

Note: Anonymization permanently removes all personal identifiers, making it impossible to link data back to individuals.

4. Your Rights

  • Access: Request a copy of all recordings associated with your account
  • Deletion: Request deletion of specific or all recording data
  • Opt-Out: Opt-out of AI training use (contact privacy@lyrha.com)
  • Download: Download transcripts and summaries in portable format

5. Consent from Call Recipients

As the account holder, you must:

  • Obtain consent from the elderly person (or their legal guardian) before initiating calls
  • Inform them that calls will be recorded
  • Explain the purposes of recording
  • Provide them with the option to opt-out
"This call may be recorded for quality and service purposes."
— Announced at the beginning of each call

6. Withdrawal of Consent

You may withdraw consent at any time by:

  • Disabling recording in app settings (stops new recordings)
  • Deleting your account (removes all recordings)
  • Contacting privacy@lyrha.com

Note: Withdrawing consent may limit service functionality. Call summaries and wellness reports require recording to function.
